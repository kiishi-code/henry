  • Language

    The OJE is published in English language. Thus, manuscripts should be submitted in either American or British standard. That is authors are to stick to one version of English.

  • Lenght of papper

    The length of the paper should not exceed 20 pages as paper that are more than 20 pages will not be accepted. Manuscripts submitted should be typed in double-space (including references) using A4. with wide margins.

  • Abstract The abstract should not be more than one hundred and fifty (150) words. The abstract should reflect briefly statement of the problem, purpose of the research/paper, the Methods, results and conclusions.
    Keywords Keywords should come immediately afterwards abstract, in alphabetical order and should should not exceed eight (8) keywords and should be separated using semicolon (;).

  • Tables and Figures Tables and figures should be presented using the 7th edition of APA tables and figures style.
    References Author(s) should follow the 7th edition of APA referencing style.
